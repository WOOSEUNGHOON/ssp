FSE '26, a top-tier software engineering conference held in Montreal, Canada from July 5 to 9, was attended by Ph.D. student Heedong Yang, Master's student Sunha Park, and Seogyeong Cho.

At the conference, Heedong Yang presented his paper **"SBridge"**, and Sunha Park presented her paper **"Uncovering Similar but Different Packages in PyPI and Potential Security Threats"** 👏

For more details about **SBridge: Identifying Source-to-Binary Function Similarity via Cross-Domain Control Block Matching**,
